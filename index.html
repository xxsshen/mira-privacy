Mira Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026  ·  Effective: July 9, 2026

Your photo never leaves your phone. Mira reads your face on the device to compute your scores, then discards the photo: it is never uploaded, saved, or used to identify you. There is no account and no real name. The information Mira keeps is de-identified and tied only to a random ID, and no personally identifiable information is released, even during synchronization or backup. Mira uses no third-party advertising, never tracks you across other apps or websites, never sells your data, and never trains any model on it. Product analytics look only at how the app is used, never at the content of your wellness results.

Who We Are

This app, "Mira," is provided by Xiaoxiao Shen LLC ("we," "us"). At Mira, we believe privacy is a fundamental part of personal wellness. We designed Mira to help you understand changes in your daily well-being while keeping you in control of your information. The information Mira keeps is de-identified, it is not linked to your name, email, or any real-world identity, and no personally identifiable information is released, even during synchronization or backup. Questions? Email xiaoxioashenllc@gmail.com.

Our Privacy Principles

Our privacy practices are built around four commitments:

  • Data Minimization: We collect only the de-identified information needed to provide Mira's features.
  • Personal Experience: Your information is used to provide your own wellness history and personalized insights.
  • Transparency and Control: You can view and delete your information at any time.
  • Security: We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your information.

Consent

The first time you open Mira, a blocking "Your Privacy Comes First" screen appears with a checkbox, "I have read and agree to the Mira Privacy Notice," and two buttons: Not Now and Agree & Continue. Choosing Agree & Continue records your consent and lets you use Mira; choosing Not Now keeps the screen up, so you agree to this notice in order to use the app. With your permission, Mira securely stores your de-identified wellness history so you can view trends, recover your information if needed, and receive personalized insights. To improve Mira, we look only at how the app is used, never the content of your wellness results. You can review this Privacy Notice at any time in Profile.

Your Photo Is Never Collected

When you take a photo, Mira reads it on your device to work out your scores, and then the photo is discarded right away. Your photo is never uploaded and never stored.

No Account, No Real Name

Mira has no login, no email, and no password. You, and any family members you add, are known only by a random, de-identified ID that has no real-world identity and cannot be traced back to you. The names you type for family members are friendly labels handled the same de-identified way as your own information.

Information We Collect

When you choose to use Mira, we save only de-identified information necessary to provide Mira's features. This falls into two separate categories.

To provide your own wellness history (stored for you):

  • Your wellness scores and summaries
  • Your de-identified skin observations. Your photo is never stored.
  • The date and time of each wellness session

To understand how the app is used (product analytics):

  • How you use the app, which features you open, time spent, buttons used, and the app version
  • Device and diagnostic information needed to improve reliability and performance

Our product analytics record only how the app is used, not what your results were.

This information is tied only to a random, de-identified ID. It is not associated with your name, email address, or any information that could reasonably identify you.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Generate your personal wellness insights
  • Track changes in your wellness over time
  • Display your wellness history and trends
  • Restore your information if you recover your account
  • Improve the reliability, performance, and features of Mira using information about how the app is used, not the content of your wellness results
  • Improve your personalized experience within the app

Sync and Backup

To help protect your information, Mira may securely synchronize your wellness records across your devices or store encrypted backups in the cloud when needed. Only de-identified records are synchronized or backed up, and they are used only to restore your own wellness history, never for product analytics or model training. No personally identifiable information is transmitted or released in this process.

This allows you to recover your wellness history if you reinstall the app, replace your device, or accidentally delete local data. Cloud storage is used only to provide backup and synchronization services.

We Do Not Train Models on Your Data

We do not use the content of your data to train or improve our models. We do not use your de-identified information to train or improve any model.

Information Sharing

Your data is not shared. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share your wellness information with advertisers or use it for third-party advertising or marketing.

We disclose information only: when you request or authorize us to do so; when necessary to provide the services you use; or when required by applicable law.

Your Control

You remain in control of your information. You can view your wellness history, delete individual wellness records, delete your account and associated data, and withdraw your consent by discontinuing use of Mira and deleting your account.

You may also email us at xiaoxioashenllc@gmail.com to request access to, or deletion of, any information associated with your random ID.

Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or loss. De-identified records are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and kept in our own systems, tied only to a random ID.

Not a Medical Device

Mira offers gentle, everyday wellness reflections. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose. It is never a substitute for a doctor.

Children

Mira is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Any information you enter about a family member, including a child you add, is handled the same de-identified way as your own information.

Changes to This Privacy Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time as Mira evolves or as required by law. When material changes are made, we will update the effective date and notify you through the app when appropriate.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Notice or your information, please contact us at xiaoxioashenllc@gmail.com.
