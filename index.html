Mira Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026  ·  Effective: July 9, 2026

Your privacy comes first. Mira analyzes your photo on your device and then discards it, and by default your information stays on your device. This policy describes what information Mira processes and your choices. Mira is provided by Xiaoxiao Shen LLC. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising. We apply reasonable safeguards to protect your information.

Information We Process

Your selfie is analyzed on your device to compute your wellness scores and a short summary, and the photo is then discarded. Your photo is not uploaded, stored, or collected.

By default, your scores, summaries, and history are kept only in the app on your device. If you turn on the optional cloud backup, only de-identified wellness values (your scores, summaries, session dates, and skin observations, never a photo) are stored so you can restore your own history, tied only to a random ID that is not linked to your name, email, or real-world identity.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide and maintain the app, to show your wellness history and trends, to restore your history if you have turned on cloud backup, and to comply with applicable law. We do not train or improve any model using your data. We do not share your information with advertisers or use it for third-party advertising.

Data Retention and Your Choices

Information kept on your device remains until you delete it in the app or remove the app. If you turn on cloud backup, we retain those de-identified values until you turn backup off or ask us to delete them. You can view and delete your wellness records in the app, and you may contact us to request deletion of information associated with your random ID.

Not a Medical Device

Mira offers gentle, everyday wellness reflections. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose. It is never a substitute for a doctor.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When appropriate, we will update the effective date and provide notice as required by applicable law.
