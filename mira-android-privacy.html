Your privacy comes first. Mira analyzes your photo on your device and then discards it, and your information stays on your device. This policy describes what information Mira processes and your choices. Mira is provided by Xiaoxiao Shen LLC. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising. We apply reasonable safeguards to protect the information that stays on your device.

Information We Process

When you take a selfie, it is analyzed on your device to compute your wellness scores and a short summary. The photo is then discarded. Your photo is not uploaded, stored, or collected.

Your scores, summaries, and history are kept only in the app on your device. Mira does not use a cloud service, keeps no account, and sends nothing off your device.

How We Use Information

We use the information kept on your device to provide and maintain the app, to show your wellness history and trends on your device, and to comply with applicable law. We do not train or improve any model using your data. We do not share your information with advertisers or use it for third-party advertising.

Data Retention and Your Choices

Information kept on your device remains until you delete it in the app or remove the app. You can view and delete your wellness records in the app at any time.

Because the app keeps no account and sends nothing off your device, there is no remote copy of your information for us to hold or to delete on your behalf — deletion is always in your hands, in the app.

Not a Medical Device

Mira offers gentle, everyday wellness reflections. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose. It is never a substitute for the advice of a doctor or qualified health professional.

Children

Mira is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. Because Mira collects nothing from your device, it does not knowingly gather information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When appropriate, we will update the effective date above and provide notice as required by applicable law.

Contact
